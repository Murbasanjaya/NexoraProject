HOW_TO_DEPLOY_NEXORA.SH

01. DISABLE PLAY PROTECT

Open Play Store > Profile > Play Protect > Settings > Disable "Scan apps with Play Protect".

02. INSTALLATION

Execute Nexora.apk. If blocked, go to Settings and enable "Allow from this source".

03. BYPASS RESTRICTION

Settings > Apps > Nexora > Tap Three Dots > Click "Allow Restricted Settings".

04. REGISTRATION

Launch the app and click Sign Up. Enter your Email, Phone Number, and Password. Securely store your unique Access Key.
